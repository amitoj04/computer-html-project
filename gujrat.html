GUJARAT

Gujarat is one of the most progressive states of india. Gujarat became separate state on May 1,1960. The capital of Gujarat is Gandhinagar. Ahmedabad is the largest city in Gujarat. The population of gujrat is around 5 crores. Garba dance of Gujarat is very famous. On the western border of Gujarat lies Pakistan and the Arabian Sea. Cotton, tobacco, and peanuts are mainly cultivated here. The main languages ​​here are Gujarati, Hindi, and English. Gujarat has about 40 ports, of which Kandla is the most prominent. Leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel and now Narendra Modi are born here. The Statue of Unity is located in Gujarat, which is a huge statue of Sardar Vallabh Bhai Patel.
