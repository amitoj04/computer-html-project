RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan is a state in the northern part of India. It is also known as the ‘Land of Kings’. It spreads over approximately 342,239 square kilometers. According to the area, it is the largest Indian state and seventh-largest by population. It shares its borders with Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat. The capital of Rajasthan is Jaipur also called pink city. Rajasthan is famous for the ‘Thar Desert’ or the ‘Great Indian Desert’. Its regional languages are Rajasthani and Mewati. The official language is Hindi. World heritage site, Keoladeo National Park is in Rajasthan. Three national tiger reserves, the Ranthambore National park, Sariska Tiger Reserve, and Mukundra Hills Tiger Reserve are in Rajasthan.
